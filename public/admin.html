Quick Start

A simple flow for a new screen setup.
1. Upload a logo, menu PDF, image, or video under Media.
2. Set colors and slogan under Branding.
3. Build a playlist under Playlists with the visual slide builder.
4. Assign that playlist to a screen here in Overview.
5. Open /display/your-screen-name on the TV or Raspberry Pi.

Theme Preview Tips

Make signage easier to read from across a room.
Use a dark background with bright text for hallways and cafeterias.
Keep accent colors bold for alerts and menu highlights.
Use square PNG logos when possible for the cleanest top bar.
Short slogans work better than long mission statements on TVs.
